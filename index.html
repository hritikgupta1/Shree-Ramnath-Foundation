    ABOUT US

    Shree Ramnath Foundation – a beacon of service and transformation was founded in 2020 with a vision to uplift and empower underserved communities across India. Rooted in compassion and guided by purpose, we strive to bridge the gap between need and opportunity through holistic development. At Shree Ramnath Foundation, we channel collective strength into impactful action. Our focus spans across healthcare, education, women and child welfare, skill development, and environmental sustainability. With a passionate team and community-driven approach, we provide meaningful support to children, families, the elderly, and the differently-abled. We don’t just respond to issues—we anticipate, innovate, and act with empathy. From conducting free medical camps and disaster relief to abolishing social evils like child marriage and bonded labor, our initiatives aim to build a just and dignified society for all. Driven by our mission of inclusive growth and our vision of a transformed India, we collaborate with governments, corporations, and the public to amplify our reach and impact. Join us in shaping a future where every voice is heard, every life is valued, and every individual has the power to rise.

    OUR MISSION

    OUR VISION

    To build an inclusive, just, and healthy society where everyone, regardless of background, can thrive, contribute meaningfully, and live with dignity and purpose.
