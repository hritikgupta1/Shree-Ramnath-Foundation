    Since our inception in 2020, Shree Ramnath Foundation has worked to uplift marginalized communities through various impactful initiatives. We believe in action backed by compassion and transparency.

    Healthcare Camps

    Free checkups, medicines, awareness for rural and urban communities.

    Education Programs

    Supporting schools, scholarships, skills training for underprivileged students.

    Women Empowerment

    Vocational training and leadership development for women’s self-reliance.

    Skill Development

    Workshops teaching income skills to youth and women for independence.

    Clean Environment Drive

    Sanitation campaigns and eco-friendly practices to keep surroundings clean.

    Youth Engagement

    Leadership, sports, and community projects empowering young people’s futures.

    Relief Work During Disasters
    Emergency food and shelter for victims of floods, earthquakes, and pandemics.
    Old Age and Orphan Care
    We operate care centers offering food, shelter, education, and dignity to the neglected.
    Environment & Sanitation
    Tree plantation drives, water conservation awareness, and sanitation programs in local villages.
    Women & Child Welfare
    Programs focusing on nutrition, education, and safety of women and children in rural regions.
    Self-Sustainable Development
    Uplifting marginalized communities through micro-finance support and sustainable livelihood projects.
    Public Health Awareness
    Awareness campaigns on population control, hygiene, tuberculosis, and lifestyle diseases.
